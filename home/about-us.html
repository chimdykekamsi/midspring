Why Choose Midspring Bank?

Midspring Bank has been active since 2017, founded by partners Audrius Ramanauskas and Tadas Kvedaras. As we grew from a small business into a company with hundreds of clients – we had to evolve and redesign our business model – introducing a compliance-first approach to European and international banking.

Midspring Bank also enjoys working with clients who have complex or challenging business models especially those involving digital currencies – helping them regulate their international services where possible.
Midspring Bank is regulated by the bank of Australia (License no.11) and is subject to regular audits to ensure compliance with domestic and international financial laws and standards.
